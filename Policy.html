We process your data under the following legal foundations: Contractual Necessity (to deliver cloud sync and purchases) and Legitimate Interests (to maintain game health, combat fraud, and serve non-intrusive ads).

  • To manage your Traveler profile and progression metrics.
  • To provide the Offline Protection validation features.
  • To process rewards like Void Fragments and Tech Scraps.
  • To maintain the integrity and fair play of the Daily Breach rankings.

Depending on your location, you hold comprehensive rights regarding your personal data. You have the right to request access to, correction of, or erasure of any data synced with our global systems, or object to specific processing contexts. To exercise these privileges, reach out to our administrative address listed below.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA / GDPR Compliance)

We do not knowingly collect or target personal data from children under the age of 13 (or 16 in specified regional jurisdictions). If a minor is identified via our initial software age-gate mechanism, we strictly disable behavioral tracking configurations and pass an automated "child-directed" configuration flag to our ad networks to enforce child safety protocols.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Local Data: Can be permanently removed by you at any time via the "Wipe All Game Data" option inside the in-game Settings menu.
